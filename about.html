Our Mission & Vision

Mission

We are dedicated to cultivating, nurturing and fostering a spirit of faith, love and stewardship among Catholic youths.

Vision

To harvest a crop of dedicated, inspired and compassionate Catholic youths who are courageous to uphold and defend Catholic faith, to spread God’s love and advocate for environmental conservation.

Our Team

Our History

Founded in 2010, our organization has grown from a small group of 5 members to a thriving community of over 200 active participants. Over the years, we've completed numerous projects that have benefited our community and created lasting impact.
